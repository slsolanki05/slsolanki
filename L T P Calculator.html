What is L T P?

  • L (Lectures): Traditional classroom teaching hours
  • T (Tutorials): Small group problem-solving sessions
  • P (Practicals): Laboratory or hands-on sessions
  • Formula: Practical hours count as half credit (P/2) compared to lecture and tutorial hours
